About Me

My name is Herica De Casas, a Growth Marketer focused on growing sustainable, ethical, and slow-fashion businesses. If there is one thing I forever take with me is my love for ecosystems and environmentally-friendly fashions. My point is to grow sustainable businesses that I believe in.

My conviction for sustainable lifestyle brands originates in my family background. I am a Chicana, my parents are from rural Mexico and they passed down to me a respect for nature and an admiration for traditional culture. From them, I understood the exchange that occurs between living organisms. It was later in college that I learned about ecosystems and the importance of every natural resource. Alongside this Earth passion was my love for creativity and fashion. Thus, I made it my career to grow the market community of sustainable and ethical fashion brands.

The best way to retain the market and expand organically is through engaging a community. If we want to see the day where we close unsustainable cycles, it’s up to us to create the community, facilitate accessibility, and increase knowledge about sustainable practices. Environmentally friendly is ready to do business. And Fortunately, my experience and expertise in growth marketing is all about building engaging communities. I believe every individual deserves accessibility to a wonderful sustainable lifestyle- and I’m here to help make that happen.

Your Growth Marketer,
Herica
