About Me

My name is Herica De Casas, a Growth Marketer focused on enhancing sustainable, ethical, and slow-fashion businesses.
My passion is in enhancing businesses that empower their customer through sustainable, ethical, and eco-friendly garments. And I do this through growth marketing strategies. I love working for companies that I believe in, companies that lead with a mission.

I am a first generation Chicana- where my parents came from the arid regions of central rural Mexico. They gifted me my admiration for traditional cultures, a care for natural ecosystems, and a fondness for practicing sustainability. If fashion is expressing oneself, then our chosen garments should express every bit of who we are down to the buttons and textiles. Wardrobes should reflect our values, beliefs, and personal experiences. My mission is to help grow eco-friendly, ethically made, and expressively unique wardrobe businesses by expanding their market outreach. We deserve feel good choices in every aspect of our self expression.
And that is exactly what I am set out to do.

The best way to expand and retain a target market is by engaging and flourishing a genuine community. Communities spread the word, keep us close in a mission, and inspire new endeavors for making major changes in the world. As a growth marketer I help design plans for expansion, strategize for optimization, and manage platform operations. Let's build a community of change together.

Your Growth Marketer,
Herica
