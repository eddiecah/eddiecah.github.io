SEO

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is the "non-paid"(also referred to as "organic") strategy for increasing site traffic by having your web pages show up in the top results of search engines like Google. There are several layers in SEO to consider such as the number of keywords and keyphrases for relevancy, the publishing dates for accuracy, and number of backlinks for credability. There are many creative directions one can go about SEO. These are some of the most popular building blocks & methods SEO:
  • Researching Keywords & Keyphrases
  • Building a Blog Or Transcribing Podcast Audio
  • Strategizing the Content Calendar with SEO Keywords
  • Increasing Backlinks on Other Platforms
  • Creating Keywords Descriptions within Your Site's Code Mark Up

SEM

Search Engine Marketing (SEM) is the Paid Ads strategy for increasing site traffic by paying for the top spaces in Search Engine Results. SEM strategies include increasing SEO efforts, writing clear & compelling copy, and researching competitor SEM. One reason companies will dominate the SEM feild is to reduce their competitors' Ad space, and so that they may take a shot at acquiring their competitor's users. The SEM field is feisty- Here are some ways to be ahead of your competitors with SEM:
  • Maintaining High Quality SEO for your Site
  • A/B Test Ad Copy & Images
  • Purchase and Dictate Competitor Search Engine keywords
  • Managing & Regulating Ads Based Off of Live-Conversion Rates
